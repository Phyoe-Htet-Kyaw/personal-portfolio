Features

OVAL Board

OVAL Board is designed as a home place function for students to seek and read information regarding your academic life including Universities, Scholarships, Social organization, Events and Career. This is a place where students can find the academic and career opportunities up-to-the-minutes.

Any ask

This is a function where users can make a query about any information or opportunities from the diverse background of university students. The nearest possible answers are prioritized sequentially together with post voting technique for the questioners.

Community

Group Function is available on the OVAL screen to create & connect several networks of Youths based organizations and can participate in their social activities.

Universities

This feature is designated for universities detailed information including its profile, location linked with Google map, its specialized majors, ratings & reviews, students’ clubs’ information and the annual celebrations.

Talents

Talent is such an amazing place for students to express their abilities by creating millions of your vlogs where different types of creativities can be seen. Students can also write regular posts experiencing the real word formatting style in this function. Font styles, colors and alignments can be simply edited and links can also be attached which can be opened automatically without reaching to another application. This is a place where talentsis praised by millions of your peers.

Chatty

This function is especially made for students to be able to send messages to beloved ones with cute stickers,emojis and can share memorable moments into images.

Audio Call

Chatty is allowed to stay connected through voices.

Video Call

Visualizing will make you and your acquaintances more attached.
